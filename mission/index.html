Mission

Reclaiming human agency in the agentic era.

A practice for keeping human judgment at the center of creative work as AI takes over more of the making.

The claim

Agentic Creative Intelligence makes a bold claim. Rejuvenating our creative capabilities can reclaim our human agency in the agentic era.

As AI takes over more of the making, the practitioner who keeps her judgment in the work is the one who still shapes it. That judgment is a set of capabilities, and capabilities can be cultivated.

Mind, hand, and body

We open with Mens et Manus, mind and hand, and add a third term, Membra, the body. The framework runs Mens, Manus, Membra. The making hand is held at the center, where mind and body become one in the act of creation.

Two axes span a field of creative capability. The mind reaches from intention to realization. The body moves between disposition and orientation. At the center sits the design object, the thing being made, where the making activates the whole field.

Four capabilities, one capacity

Each capability foregrounds one aspect of the work, and each meets one family of AI that offers a real gift while quietly subtracting something the body knows.

Extended Engagement gives the work its Shape. It meets world models, which offer immersion and can subtract presence. The practitioner keeps her hand on the material.

Aesthetic Awareness gives the work its Style. It meets large language models, which offer fluency and can subtract the struggle that earns a voice.

Adaptive Coherence gives the work its Story. It meets causal models, which offer logic and can subtract the open inquiry that keeps a question alive.

Expressive Influence gives the work its Statement. It meets diffusion and platform tools, which offer reach and can subtract synthesis, the unforeseen connection.

Held apart, they are four. Brought together around the work, they are one capacity. That capacity is Agentic Creative Intelligence.

The dæmon

A dæmon is an AI intermediary built to develop you, rather than to finish your work. It provokes, withholds the answer, makes no mark, and recedes as the capability becomes yours. Where an ordinary agent completes the task, a dæmon keeps you in it.

Why now

Contemporary AI can feel like a Faustian bargain. A world model offers the imagination of shape and subtracts the resistance a hand earns. A language model offers the whole corpus of style and subtracts the body's slow origination. The practice is to take the gift and keep the cost in view, so the work stays yours.
